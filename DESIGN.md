# Design System

## Colors
- Background: #000000 (pure black)
- Surface: #111111 (cards, panels)
- Border: #222222 (subtle borders)
- Text Primary: #FFFFFF
- Text Secondary: #888888
- Accent: #0070F3 (blue — buttons, links, highlights)
- Accent Hover: #0060D3
- Success: #22C55E
- Error: #EF4444

## Typography
- Font: Inter, system-ui, sans-serif
- Heading 1: 48px, font-weight 700, line-height 1.1
- Heading 2: 32px, font-weight 600
- Heading 3: 20px, font-weight 600
- Body: 16px, font-weight 400, line-height 1.6
- Small: 14px, color #888888

## Spacing
- Section padding: 80px vertical
- Card padding: 24px
- Border radius: 8px (cards), 6px (buttons), 4px (inputs)
- Max content width: 1200px, centered

## Components
- Buttons: solid background #0070F3, white text, 6px radius, 12px 24px padding
- Cards: background #111111, border 1px solid #222222, 8px radius
- Inputs: background #000000, border 1px solid #333333, white text, 6px radius
- Nav: transparent background, blur backdrop, sticky top

## Style Rules
- Clean, minimal, professional — inspired by Vercel
- Generous whitespace between sections
- Subtle hover effects (opacity or border color change)
- No gradients on backgrounds — flat dark surfaces only
- Icons should be simple and monochrome
